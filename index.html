Welcome to Dawn Infinite Imagery

I'm JC, the heart and soul behind the camera. My journey into videography wasn't traditional; after years of exploring various paths and never quite feeling settled, I found what I'd been searching for in videography. At 39, I discovered I'd been living with undiagnosed ADHD, which explained my lifelong search for creativity, excitement, and freedom. Then, I picked up a camera, and suddenly, everything clicked.

With videography, my mind is free to roam, explore, and create without limits. Whether capturing an emotional wedding ceremony, the energy of a live concert, or a spontaneous heartfelt moment, it's never the same experience twice, and that's exactly why I love it. Dawn Infinite Imagery is my dream brought to life, a place where authenticity, individuality, and diversity shine through every project. I thrive on genuine human connection, capturing the moments that matter most, and translating them into visual stories filled with warmth, emotion, and artistry.

For me, it's not just about clients; by the time we finish a project, we've become friends. The greatest reward is seeing your vision come to life and witnessing the excitement in your eyes when you first view your story captured on film. Whether it's your wedding day, a live event, or any personal project, Dawn Infinite Imagery is here to ensure your unique story is told exactly how it deserves to be—authentically and beautifully.

Together, let's bring your cinematic vision to reality.
