Gear & Skills

As a third-year Digital Filmmaking student at LA Film School, I have developed a strong foundation in cinematography, editing, and storytelling**. My skills continue to evolve as I push my creative limits and master new techniques. Below is a list of the gear I use and the skills that define my craft.

🎥 Camera Gear

  • Blackmagic Pocket Cinema Camera 4K – High-quality 4K video production.
  • iPhone 16 Pro (Blackmagic iOS App) – On-the-go shooting with pro controls.
  • DJI OM 4 Gimbal – Smooth cinematic stabilization.
  • Manfrotto MVH502A Fluid Head Tripod – Professional motion and stability.
  • ND Filters & Diffusion – Controlling light for creative exposure.

🎙️ Audio Equipment

  • Rode Shotgun Mic – Crisp directional audio for film.
  • Zoom H6 Handy Recorder – High-quality multi-track audio recording.
  • Rode Wireless Lapel Mic – Clear and mobile audio capture.
  • Sennheiser & Sony Studio Headphones – Precise audio monitoring.

💡 Lighting & Accessories

  • Aputure Amaran 100x – Powerful and versatile lighting.
  • Aputure Light Dome II – Softbox for cinematic lighting.
  • RGB Camera Lights – Creative lighting for unique shots.
  • Lighting Gels & Diffusers – Customizable color effects.

🖥️ Editing & Post-Production

  • Adobe Premiere Pro 24 – Professional video editing.
  • DaVinci Resolve Studio 19 – Color grading and advanced editing.
  • Logic Pro 11 – Audio mixing and post-production.
  • Adobe Photoshop & After Effects – Motion graphics & image enhancement.

🎬 Essential Skills

These are the **core skills that shape my work as a videographer and editor**:

🔥 Cinematography & Camera Work

  • Understanding **composition, framing, and visual storytelling**.
  • Mastering **camera movement** for dynamic shots (handheld, gimbal, sliders).
  • Controlling **depth of field and exposure** for cinematic aesthetics.

🎞️ Editing & Post-Production

  • Precision **video cutting, pacing, and sequencing**.
  • Advanced **color grading in DaVinci Resolve**.
  • Creating **seamless transitions and visual effects**.
  • Syncing and mixing **audio for professional sound design**.

🎭 Directing & Storytelling

  • Developing **compelling narratives and shot lists**.
  • Guiding **actors and on-screen talent** to achieve the best performances.
  • Executing **pre-production planning & creative direction**.

⚡ On-Set Workflow & Production

  • Managing **camera settings, lenses, and lighting on set**.
  • Collaborating with **directors, producers, and crew members**.
  • Ensuring a **smooth shooting workflow with backups and organization**.

📈 Continuous Learning & Growth

Filmmaking is an **ever-evolving craft**. As a student at **LA Film School**, I am constantly honing my skills, staying ahead of **industry trends, new camera tech, and evolving editing techniques**. This journey is about **constant improvement, experimentation, and pushing creative boundaries.**
